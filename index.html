About Us

The Biblical Empowerment Program (B.E.P) is a faith-driven initiative dedicated to equipping individuals with a deeper understanding of the Word of God and the transformative power of the Holy Spirit. Our mission is to build spiritually grounded believers who are empowered to live purposeful, Christ-centered lives.

At B.E.P, we believe that true empowerment comes through knowledge, revelation, and application of Scripture. Through structured teachings, interactive sessions, and Spirit-led discussions, we create a space where individuals can grow in faith, gain clarity in their spiritual journey, and develop a personal relationship with God.

Our program is designed for everyone—whether you are a new believer or someone seeking deeper spiritual insight. We focus on practical teachings that address real-life challenges while guiding individuals to walk in faith, wisdom, and obedience to God’s will.

We are committed to:

  • Teaching the Word of God with clarity and truth
  • Empowering believers through the Holy Spirit
  • Building a strong, united faith community
  • Encouraging spiritual growth and personal transformation

The Biblical Empowerment Program is more than just a teaching platform—it is a movement of believers rising in faith, purpose, and power.

Thursday Sessions

The Thursday sessions are held every week from 7:00 PM to 8:30 PM. They are designed to deepen participants’ understanding of the Word of God through structured teaching and interactive engagement.

The goal is to build a strong biblical foundation while encouraging spiritual growth and practical application of Scripture in daily life. Each session is centered on presentation-based learning, where topics are shared and explored through discussion, comments, and questions, promoting active participation.

These sessions aim to create a space for the diverse expansion of biblical knowledge, allowing participants to learn through Scripture, guided teaching, and shared insights.

Objectives

  • Strengthening biblical understanding and spiritual maturity
  • Encouraging active participation through questions and discussion
  • Promoting clear and correct interpretation of Scripture
  • Building confidence in sharing and applying biblical knowledge
  • Fostering spiritual growth through collective learning
