About B.E.P

Building and empowering believers through the Word, discipleship, and spiritual growth.

Who We Are

The Biblical Empowerment Program (B.E.P) is a Christ-centered ministry dedicated to equipping believers with sound biblical knowledge, spiritual growth, and practical empowerment for victorious Christian living. We exist to build, strengthen, and empower the Body of Christ through the teaching of the Word, prayer, and discipleship.

Our Mission

To empower believers with solid biblical teaching that inspires faith, deepens understanding, and produces spiritual growth, so they can live a purposeful and victorious Christian life.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of Spirit-filled, Word-grounded believers who influence their families, communities, and the world with the power of the Gospel.

What We Do

• Weekly Online Bible Empowerment Sessions (Every Thursday – 19:00 to 20:30)
• Teaching, Prayer & Biblical Discussions
• Equipping believers with practical Christian principles
• Building a community of spiritual growth and encouragement
