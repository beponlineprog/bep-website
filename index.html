About Us

Biblical Empowerment Program (B.E.P) is a Christ-centered online ministry dedicated to strengthening believers through the Word of God, prayer, and spiritual teaching.

We exist to build, equip, and empower the Church of Christ by making biblical teachings accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our weekly online sessions are held every Thursday from 19:00 to 20:30.

Our Mission

To empower believers through sound biblical teaching, spiritual growth, and practical Christian living.

Our Vision

To see a globally empowered Church grounded in the truth of God’s Word.

What We Offer

  • Weekly online Bible teaching sessions
  • Prayer and spiritual guidance
  • Faith-building discussions
  • A growing Christian community
